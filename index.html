Hello world...

My name is Emma Lozhkin and this is my website! I am currently studying Computer Engineering at the University of Waterloo.
I just finished my first co-op as a Software Developer at Nymi Inc.

I am a 2017 Schulich Leader Scholarship Award recipient for the University of Waterloo, and am very grateful to have been given this opportunity to pursue my studies in Computer Engineering.

I am an Olympian and a member of the Canadian National Gymnastics team between 2012-2017, and have represented Canada at many international competitions. The competitive environment I grew up in has helped me to develop a very strong work ethic and the ability to overcome challenges.

Through high academic achievements, international-level athletics, and leadership experiences, I have stimulated my intellectual vitality and learned to thrive in challenging environments. I embrace every chance to explore and foster my intellectual creativity.
